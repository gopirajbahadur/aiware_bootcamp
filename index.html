About The Event

AIware Bootcamp

Zero to Hero in 5 days. A begineer friendly, hand-on focussed AIware bootcamp. Aimed at educating software makers and researchers at any expertise level to learn more about how to build, ship and research on AIware. Come join us to learn and remake the future of software!
